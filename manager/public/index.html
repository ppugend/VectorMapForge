📖 Viewer URL Guide (click to expand)
Method 1 — lat/lng
/viewer.html?lat=lat&lng=lng&zoom=zoom  — no region needed. Roads visible at zoom 14–16.
Seoul /viewer.html?lat=37.504364&lng=127.051338&zoom=15
Monaco /viewer.html?lat=43.735800&lng=7.421300&zoom=15
Bangkok /viewer.html?lat=13.753620&lng=100.490060&zoom=15
Method 2 — ZXY tile coordinate
/viewer.html?z=z&x=x&y=y  — centers on the tile. No region needed.
Seoul /viewer.html?z=15&x=27948&y=12696 (선릉역)
Monaco /viewer.html?z=15&x=17059&y=11948
Bangkok /viewer.html?z=15&x=25530&y=15119
